My integrator project

Why a website on vaping?

I was looking to incorporate one of my passions, programming, into a school assignment. Of course, the opportunities were limited since the majority of our work was in paper form. However, the integrative project, which offers students a wider variety of choices for work, presented itself in secondary 4. It was the perfect opportunity to practice my coding skills. On top of that, I was able to have several people deliver a message about a situation that concerns me a lot: vaping.
